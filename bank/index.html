How to copy image link for bank verification

Please follow these instructions to be verified within Bank Death Ball clan.

here are the steps to upload and copy an image link on Discord:

  1. Open the Discord app and navigate to the channel or chat where you want to upload your image.

  2. Drag your image into the chat or click the ‘+’ button to upload a file.

  3. Press enter to post the image.

  4. Once the image is uploaded and visible in your chat, right-click on the image and select 'Copy link.

If you’re using Discord on mobile, the steps are slightly different:

  1. Open the Discord app and update it if needed.

  2. Go to your profile and scroll down to behavior

  3. Enable developer mode.

  4. Go back and select an image you want to copy the link of.

  5. Press and hold the image and tap on share.

  6. Tap on copy to clipboard.

Please note that these steps may vary slightly depending on the version of Discord you’re using. If you’re having trouble, I recommend checking out these video tutorials for more detailed instructions. Enjoy Bank!
